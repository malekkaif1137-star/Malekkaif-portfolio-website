— How to Measure Your Foot
📄
Step 1 — Prepare
Place a blank sheet of paper on a hard flat floor. Stand on it with your heel touching a wall.
✏️
Step 2 — Trace
Trace the outline of your foot with a pencil held vertically. Do both feet — use the larger measurement.
📏
Step 3 — Measure
Measure the longest distance from heel to toe in centimetres. Match to the CM column above.
— Brand-Specific Fit Notes
Nike
Generally true to size. Air Max styles tend to run slightly long — consider sizing down ½.
Adidas
True to size for most models. Ultraboost runs slightly narrow — wide feet may size up.
Puma
Runs slightly small. Suede and RS-X models — size up ½ for a comfortable fit.
New Balance
True to size. Available in multiple widths (D standard, 2E wide). 990 series runs true.
Converse
Runs large — size down 1 full size from your usual UK size for the best fit.
Skechers
True to size. Memory foam models fit slightly looser — true size or ½ size down.
